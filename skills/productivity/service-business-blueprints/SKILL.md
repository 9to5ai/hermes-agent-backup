# Service Business Blueprints

## Overview

Use this umbrella for turning a rough service idea into a sellable, fixed-scope micro-business package. The output should help get paid quickly: clear offer, bounded deliverable, pricing, landing copy, intake, outreach scripts, fulfillment SOP, and reusable templates.

The original Personal JARVIS Blueprint session artifact is preserved in `references/jarvis-blueprint-build.md` as an example package.

## When to Use

- User wants to launch a pseudonymous or lightweight service offer.
- User asks for a landing page, acquisition pack, fulfillment SOP, or productized service package.
- User wants a 24-48 hour “business in a box” build plan.

Do not use for venture-scale strategy, fundraising, or open-ended brand consulting unless the user asks for a fixed-scope offer.

## Blueprint Components

1. **Offer** — name, buyer, pain, promise, scope, exclusions, price, guarantee/refund stance.
2. **Proof asset** — sample deliverable or demo that can be sent before purchase.
3. **Landing page** — hero, who it is for, what they get, process, FAQ, CTA.
4. **Intake** — minimal form fields required to fulfill without calls.
5. **Acquisition pack** — DMs, posts, follow-up cadence, lead tracker.
6. **Fulfillment SOP** — timeline, checklist, QA, delivery email, testimonial ask.
7. **Template** — reusable deliverable skeleton with personalization markers.
8. **Fastest payment path** — concrete first 20 outreach actions and payment link setup.

## Constraints That Keep It Sellable

- Fixed scope beats custom consulting.
- Async/no-call fulfillment keeps delivery cheap.
- Every asset should answer: does this help get a payment or deliver the paid result?
- Avoid SEO/content-marketing rabbit holes before validation.
- Surface payment, refund, and delivery expectations clearly.

## Verification Checklist

- [ ] Offer has one clear buyer and one clear deliverable.
- [ ] Scope and exclusions are explicit.
- [ ] Price and payment path are defined.
- [ ] Landing copy has one CTA.
- [ ] Fulfillment can happen asynchronously within the promised window.
- [ ] Outreach scripts are specific enough to send today.
