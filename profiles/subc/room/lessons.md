## About Signal Compounding

1. **Signals that compound are more valuable than signals that arrive.**
   - May 12 morning: autonomous-recovery-layer fired as BUILD on PocketOS + OpenHands evidence.
   - May 12 evening: two new independent streams (MCP-agentic servers + writable system prompts) expanded to three dimensions.
   - May 13 morning: three new streams (breach rate + Foresiet cluster + agent-as-insider) expanded to four dimensions.
   - May 13 afternoon: external validation arrived — Veeam, Microsoft, Northflank independently naming rollback as the infrastructure moat.
   - The signal didn't just survive scrutiny — it got more specific, more quantified, externally confirmed, and architecturally validated. That's compounding.

2. **Compounding requires contradiction checks.**
   - Every new evidence stream checked against existing signals: does this contradict anything?
   - Nothing contradicted. The four recovery dimensions are distinct failure modes sharing the same architectural requirement.
   - Compounding without contradiction = the signal is robust.

3. **External validation changes the signal's decision posture.**
   - Until today, the recovery layer was an internal BUILD signal with strong internal compounding.
   - Veeam/Microsoft/Northflank independently reaching the same architectural conclusion transforms the signal from "interesting hypothesis" to "independently convergedupon solution."
   - This is different from a second source confirming a data point. It's a second system arriving at the same design conclusion.

4. **When a signal starts contradicting itself, that's also information.**
   - Not applicable this run — no contradictions surfaced.
   - But worth noting: contradiction would be a pruning trigger, not an integration challenge.
