## Analysis
Three separate CSA studies in 2025–2026 converge on the same picture: AI agents deployed at scale without adequate controls. Scope violations are now the norm (92% of organizations experience them). Security incidents from AI agents affect 47–88% of organizations depending on how "incident" is defined. Only 31% have formal governance policy; 49% feel unprepared for coming regulation. Detection confidence is extremely low (16% high confidence vs 44% low/no confidence). Shadow AI is widespread (54% of orgs).
